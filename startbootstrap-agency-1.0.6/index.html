Info

Info about our product and offer.

Subscription

We only sell our pods via a subscription. The reason we do this is simply because we don't have any stock, and send the pods to you directly after they are packaged.

Fresh Coffee

Our coffee is always freshly rosted and grinded, directly after that they are packed into pods and nitrogen seeled. This way the coffee won't oxidate.

Single Estate

Our coffee is single estate this means that the coffee comes from a collective of locale farmer we trust. Thereby we can asure high quality and great work conditions for the workers.
